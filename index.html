Who is Sheikh Samir Ali Affan?


      

        Sheikh Samir Ali Affan is a Bangladeshi cybersecurity specialist, ethical hacker,
        web developer, and programmer, actively working to strengthen digital safety and
        cybersecurity awareness across Bangladesh and South Asia.
      


      

        He works as an advanced cybersecurity researcher and ethical hacking professional,
        focusing on protecting digital identities, securing web systems, and mitigating
        modern cyber threats through ethical and responsible practices.
      


      

        His expertise includes ethical hacking, vulnerability assessment, penetration
        testing, secure coding, phishing and scam analysis, website security testing,
        and cybersecurity awareness initiatives.
      


      

        He actively works against spam, phishing, fake accounts, impersonation,
        digital harassment, and online fraud, and assists individuals and organizations
        with account recovery and digital risk prevention.
      


      

        He is the founder of BanglaSafe, a digital safety initiative
        dedicated to cybersecurity awareness, online protection, and responsible use
        of technology in Bangladesh.
